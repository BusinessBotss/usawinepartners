The Hand Behind the Vision
Travis Braithwaite
Crafting · Direction · Wine Identity

Born in Canada and raised in South Africa, Travis Braithwaite has shaped a body of work defined by aesthetic sensitivity, international ambition and a precise understanding of how to build wines with identity, depth and the capacity to move people.

He does not work by denomination. He works by harmony in the glass — and that philosophy defines every bottle within this proposal.

  • A globally acclaimed winemaker
  • Recipient of international awards across multiple continents
  • Renowned for mastering complex, multi-country blends
  • Creative force behind some of the most original modern wine projects in circulation
More than making wine, Travis treats each label as a work of fine construction — balance, character, journey, and a clear idea of contemporary luxury.

Nip Slip

Red · Shiraz · Cabernet Sauvignon · Mourvèdre

General Impression

Nip Slip is an iconic red blend that captures a transformative journey — from playful youth to graceful sophistication. Built on a Stellenbosch core with international varietal influence, it shows ripe red and black fruit, subtle spice, a silky entry, structured mid-palate and polished tannins. The wine with the greatest commercial flexibility within this proposal — leaving room for incentives, activation, staff engagement and volume-driven performance.

02
White · Chenin Blanc · Viognier · Chardonnay
Second Wine · A White With Real Depth

Grace 2020

Chenin Blanc 60% · Viognier 30% · Chardonnay 10%

General Impression

A white of serious ambition — built not to dazzle through easy freshness, but through depth, texture and evolution. Citrus and peach lift on the nose, with vanilla and pronounced lees character. The Chenin gives spine and structure; the Viognier adds floral lift and an enveloping mouthfeel. This is not a fragile white. It does not fade between vintages — it holds, evolves and gains depth. A natural candidate for high-rotation signature white without ever compromising elegance.

Third Wine · Presence & Prestige

Pangea

A True Wine of the World · Limited Edition

General Impression

Pangea is conceived from an extraordinary idea: a true Wine of the World, assembled from noble varieties grown in some of the planet's greatest origins. Cabernet Sauvignon from Napa Valley, Merlot from Right Bank Bordeaux, Petit Verdot from Spain, Cabernet Franc from South Africa and Malbec from Argentina. Complex yet seamless. Layered yet restrained. Not designed for volume — designed for presence and prestige. Its role is to elevate the entire wine program by association.

Fifth Wine · Mineral Precision

Sauvignon Blanc 2018

100% Sauvignon Blanc · Lightly oaked · Site-driven

General Impression

A unique Sauvignon Blanc, a reflection of site and soil. South-facing 16-year-old vineyards sitting 175m above sea level, on deep Hutton and Clovelly soils. Lightly oaked, displaying minerality with well-balanced natural acids — combining herbaceous notes with integrated tropical fruit: lime zest, green apple and pineapple. An elegant freshness with a long, crisp finish — perfect with fresh oysters, grilled fish, Asian-inspired dishes and sushi.

Incentives & Flexibility

No fixed structures

First we identify which wines and brands are of real interest. From there, the commercial framework is built together.

01
Service-staff cashback
2.5% cashback for service staff based on menu price — a direct, motivating mechanic that aligns the floor team with the wines they recommend.
02
Venue-level rebates
Sales-based rebates structured around real performance — a transparent mechanism that grows in value as the partnership grows.
03
Experience-led rewards
Private tastings, professional wine training, experiential rewards and curated wine cases — built for the people who carry the program every day.
04
An open conversation
There are no fixed structures. Every framework — commercial, operational, creative — is shaped to fit the venue, not imposed onto it.
